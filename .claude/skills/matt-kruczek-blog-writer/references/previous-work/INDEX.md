# Previous Work Index

This folder contains Matt Kruczek's published articles. Use these as reference material when writing new content to match his voice, structure, and quality level.

## How to Use These References

- **Before writing:** Scan 2-3 articles closest to the new topic to absorb Matt's voice and approach.
- **During writing:** Reference specific articles for structural patterns, data handling, and tone calibration.
- **During editing:** Compare draft against published work to catch AI-isms that don't match Matt's style.
- **For continuity:** Check previous articles to avoid repeating the same statistics, examples, or frameworks without adding new insight.

## Structural Patterns to Maintain

1. **Executive Read** always present with "If you only have a minute, here's what you need to know."
2. **Opening hooks** always use specific data, not generic statements.
3. **Author bio** always ends the article with LinkedIn CTA.
4. **References** section with numbered citations and URLs.
5. **Implementation guidance** always includes concrete timelines or phases.
